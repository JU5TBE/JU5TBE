Flow Flow is a book that primarily focuses how we can find happiness in our lives. The book argues that for there to be happiness one must spend their time in flow (which is a state of complete involvement and no distractions which ties to essentiallism) Main Concepts •Flow is the state in which pleasure is archieved whenyou dissociate with worldly worries and completely focus one the task or moment •Archieving a state of flow requires an activity in which (1. it has a skill level and its easy to gain feeback on actions 2. what you are doing is challenging enough to be exciting yet not too difficult to be overwhelming) •Regularily archieving this state can provide lifelong happiness and contentment even through normal chores, it can also lead to highly productive work as an additional benefit.
